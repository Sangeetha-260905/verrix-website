A team that treats your product like our own

Founded in 2014, Verrix has grown from a two-person consultancy into a full-stack digital partner trusted by companies across fintech, healthcare, and logistics.

our_story

From a garage prototype to a 40-person studio

Verrix started when our founders, tired of watching promising products die in overloaded internal IT queues, set out to build a studio that could move at a founder's pace while holding an enterprise's standard of quality.

Twelve years later, that principle still shapes every engagement: small senior teams, direct communication, and a refusal to hand your roadmap off to a call center.

Craft over headcount

We stay intentionally small — every engineer on your project has shipped production software for at least five years.
